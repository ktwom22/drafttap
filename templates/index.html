About Draft Tap

Draft Tap is a proprietary sports analytics terminal developed in Lebanon, Maine. It leverages Python-based linear programming to solve the knapsack problem for DFS. Developed by Kyle Twombley, it optimizes for highest success likelihood in DraftKings NBA and MLB slates.
